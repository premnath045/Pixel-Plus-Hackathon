Frequently asked questions about KAYAK

  • How do I find travel deals on KAYAK?

    Simply use one of our travel search engines to scan for prices gathered from hundreds of travel sites. KAYAK's search results pages have loads of filter options to help you find deals, discover exactly what you're looking for and make booking seamless. Plus, there's no extra fee from KAYAK.

  • What makes KAYAK a great travel app?

    The KAYAK app puts all our flight, hotel and car hire search tools in your pocket. Find great deals, set Price Alerts, access mobile-only discounts and enjoy useful travel tools like flight tracking, price forecasts and more - all on the go.

  • How can I use KAYAK to manage my travel bookings?

    Create a free KAYAK account or download our app to access Trips - your personal travel planner. Forward your booking confirmations to trips@kayak.com and we'll organize all your plans in one place. Share itineraries, get notifications about delays and gate changes, and access helpful tools like maps and weather forecasts.

  • What are KAYAK Price Alerts?

    KAYAK Price Alerts are a free tool that tracks prices for specific flights, hotels or car hires. Set up an alert and we'll email you when the price changes or reaches the level you want. It's an easy way to monitor prices and book when the time is right.
